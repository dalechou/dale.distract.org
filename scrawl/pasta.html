Pasta

Mix and knead the following ingredients. This is good for three people.

120 g of semolina flour from Bob's Red Mill
120 g of unbleached all-purpose flour from Bob's Red Mill
1 tsp of salt
1 egg
water, if needed

Make a 'volcano' to add the egg. Apply water as needed.

Fold everything into a rough spherical dough. Cover it. Allow it to rest for 15 min.

Cut the rested dough into four parts, then flatten each part with a rolling pin (keep unflatten dough covered). Cut each part into fettuccelle-like sizes.

Boil a pot of water with another tsp of salt. Cook for 5 min after water is boiled.

Excellent with seafood. We sometimes use Trader Joe's seafood blend, which has shrimp, calamari rings, and bay scallops.
