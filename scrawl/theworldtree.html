The World Tree

There once was a tree so magnificent, so beautiful that people called it the World Tree.

The babies would sit under the tree or on top of the branches. They would cover their faces with leaves and dream about the world and talk and laugh.

One of the babies, Mobos, was so in love with the tree that he decided to take it for himself. He made up a story about a better tree, and tricked everyone to search for that tree.

Everyone set off. All the babies left the World Tree to go on the adventure. They travelled for a long time, but of course, they could not find the non-existent tree.

Meanwhile, Mobos took advantage of the situation and took over.

When everyone returned from the futile journey, they were furious. They cried under the World Tree, 'Mobos, why did you lie?'

Mobos thought hard and knew that he was wrong. He let everyone returned to the World Tree.

The tiniest babies were still wary from what had happened. They could not trust Mobos. They would not play with him.

But through games and talks and a very long time, they finally forgave Mobos.

All the babies were finally able to enjoy and share the World Tree together again.
