I cannot wish you well

I cannot wish you well, but 
wouldn't wish you ill either.
Your perfect place is the in between, the right-around-the-
     corner -- similar
     to the limbo you put me through.
     It is the counterfeit, the sustenance
day-to-day, munchable, 
but never sate.  Just enough to get
by another perishable day.

Dale Chou
2020-10-28
