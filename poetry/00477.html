Transcript of Cori's poem on the bus
—dedicated to Cori

i

Around the world
the people are different. 
Colours are different.
They wear different things; things 
are different.

Everyone likes different things.
All the people like 
different things
and different colours.

ii

Around the world—the planets—
I like to sleep while they look for it.

Wiggly-woggly seven friends—
all different colours. 
I want to see the colours and the
different planets. 

A long long time ago, I wanted to 
find a place and sleep 
on the planets.

Cori and Dale Chou
