In this apartment

In this apartment, everything is taken.  
Only the silhouettes remain.  My heart
stays at B3.  I can see the beams
of good will extending far and dark
like roots at this level.  I am going
to be here for the rest of my life.  
My turn will also come to extend
and transmute into the foundation, 
collected by the hollow, presented
side-by-side, by the bright windows.  

Dale Chou
2021-12-25
