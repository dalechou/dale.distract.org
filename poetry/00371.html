I pass through worlds

I pass through worlds so easily.

From one paradigm to another, 
I am a superhero. In each world I hold a different name: 

Hunter, Alchemist, Truth seeker,

The Inexorable Contender, He Who Does
Espionage Missions in Multinational Corporations (i.e., the Big Bads)

And Still Remains Implacable, Incorrigible 

Romantic, Misgiving Man,
He Who Looks Beyond Present Circumstances and Turns

The Labels Night After Night

Dale Chou
