Limbo

Thoughts slumberous
And would not dream. 
Words weighty 
Like statuettes

In their stillness—
They stood
In each other's way, 
Frozen by their own umbrae.

And so—woe! Stark sun! 
How my pen wept
Lines; remained barren; 
And would not wake!

Dale Chou
2004-03-10
