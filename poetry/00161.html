An entreaty

I indite ... on this paper napkin with
Certain importunity

            Of the children outside
Frolicking on the field, the noises of
The short bus, the nonchalance windows
Sheeny like shards; and the journalist
Typing by the window, taking her drink
With a whole new world

                For reasons incog.

Dale Chou
2006-01-19
