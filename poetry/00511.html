Happy mundane Sunday

We had sushi for lunch, 
coffee at the doughnut shop. 
My wife met her friend and 
they spoke for ten minutes. 
I bought a book with silver cover 
that could be scratched off 
like old paint. And so we did, 
my daughter and I together 
with a single silver coin.

Dale Chou
2022-04-03
