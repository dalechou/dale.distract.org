I am almost transparent now

I am almost transparent now.
33.  Clean.
Employed.
Paternal figure, however inexperienced.

A gentle character
Most of the time, but still afflicted by most
Noteworthy affections.

Still writing -- resolutely -- incompletely.
Oh I have little doubt
That you could see right through me.  One chance,
And you'll lightly cast me
Into the pit of this world.

But know this: I am still wearing that
Mud-coloured coat

Dale Chou
2015-05-07
