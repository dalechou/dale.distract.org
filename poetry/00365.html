Sometimes I have romantic thoughts

Sometimes I have romantic thoughts.
They don't make a lot of sense, which makes them gruesome. 
For example: if I should perish, and they
My failed rescuers
Should discover a white band close to the base of my finger, 
Cut deep into me because it was 
Where I had previously promised 
For the act of preservation central to some bizarre rescue
That I could never by myself remove -- 
Where allowances were made;
They would do it for me.

Dale Chou
2015-06-14
