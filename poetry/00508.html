Sunday morning forecast

We are in a good place now. We are not always 
here, but here we are 
today, leaning back in our rotund state 
comfy and fat at Second Floor, 
planning ahead for next week 
and the week after: 
insurance; car maintenance; 
shopping for a new sofa; 
buying bookshelves for our lesser read books; 
sipping hot coffee in the cold 
Sunday morning 
with a healthy amount of mortal optimism.

Dale Chou
2022-02-06
