Snowed under

I had never seen
Such fierce snow
On the epoxy floor.

The man in white
Was cornered
By steel and powder.

'Go away, please!' 
He said, 'But come back
In a minute.

I should be ready
For you then,
For my rescue.'

Dale Chou
2015-11-30
