The antecedent

You have
    Once again intruded—

Disguised yourself as one
Of the settings: a glass vessel of life-size
Enormity—poising from your glam back

Smooth neck questioning
    Like one transparent swan.

How you used to adore
The lesser colours—to guard and to
    Encompass? and when all's condign

To let go?

Dale Chou
2006-01-08
