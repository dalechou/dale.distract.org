This fire that burns I keep alone

This fire that burns I keep alone;
But where is the light?—
There is no darker tomorrow.

I seek refuge in the cicada songs;
Directions in the summer wind—
But where the cicadas join their palms—
A speck of shadow, one hollow sound.

Dale Chou
2009-03-14
