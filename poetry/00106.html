The thirst

The young girls wore red
Sashes of dress blotted
Circular dark—lush fiery lips
And sloppy kisses—wet shoes on
Waking streets of ash. 
Everyone's colourful under the sun.

I devoured the city's irregular

Dream
As everyone walked on.

Dale Chou
2003-02-10
