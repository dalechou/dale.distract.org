OK

I am so glad you are not OK,
but I still don't want you to die

anyway. Pick up your green duvet.
Stay warm. Stay hydrated.

Eat something.
You have come a long way

with your dissolute company.
You must sleep. Dream

long enough to meet another 
day.

Dale Chou
2019-08-30
