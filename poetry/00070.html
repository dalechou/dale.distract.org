A gift

Patient like a reader --
You have carefully placed
Soft murmurs on my lips
As I whispered your name in
Restful nights of 
Nocturnes, quiet sounds, and light in disguise. 
They have led ways to far away places, 
And made beds for brooding emotions --
These things revealed growing dependence, 
And that was a frightening thing indeed! 
Nevertheless, there was also something 
More convincing than fear -- my love 
That dared to depend again -- 
On you --
And for that 
Nights like tonight would stir peacefully
Time and again, with your company.

Dale Chou
2001-12-01
