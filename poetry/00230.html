It is not that

It is not that 
I prefer the old tongue. 
The mellow words 
Ripe like fruits. 
The clumsy (but intricate) tools. 

The aged music of 
Yesteryear. The used. The tested. 
The tried and tempted --
Beautiful things in life 
That did not pick up and go. No,

Tell me: what is there to love 
In today's minutiae? 
Marked for temporality, advertised
Like a public vehicle heading
Towards the end of this world.

Dale Chou
2012-07-09
