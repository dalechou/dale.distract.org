Forms

I hate filling in the forms. 
It is not hard, but 

I hate it. 
Name, family and first. 

And former. Nationality. 
Marital status. Date 

of birth. Date of issue. 
Expiry. 

Passport number. Some ID 
or another. 

Address 
both current and permanent. 

Telephone. 
Fax. The photo should be 

fastened here with a clip. 
The copies kept 

with this sheet. Letters 
and numbers folded 

neatly into strips 
and strips 

of boxes. Check this. Sign 
this. Next page.

Dale Chou
2019-09-17
