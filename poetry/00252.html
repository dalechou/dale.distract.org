Part of the whole

The complementary piece is
The one that fits 
To close a void, but at the same time 
Refuses all possibility of corrugation 

Because its ambit presents 
Both a tender embrace and 
A sovereign of cold independence 
That would only neighbour 
And not fold.

Dale Chou
2012-09-18
