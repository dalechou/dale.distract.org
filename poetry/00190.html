The thunder

The thunder the plunder the sounder shrouds us
Over the city with white fluff of rain
Under the grey roof now slickish black
Like one accidental platform
Beyond its predilection

Hangs its mighty mortar
Electrifying
And delivers
Every time
It goes

Boom!

Dale Chou
2007-12-03
