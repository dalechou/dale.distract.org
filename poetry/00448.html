The girl in green

She cupped the phone 
with her happy hands; 

her thumbs tapped 
against the screen 

in frantic speed, 
'I am so happy. 

We went to his 
secret place 

and ate many 
delicious things. 

Everything will be 
all right.'

Dale Chou
