Pineapples

I am listening to Adrienne.

I am eating pineapple.

I don't particularly enjoy pineapples,

But at least they are not watermelons.
Watermelons are my mortal enemy.

I am writing a short introduction
About myself, which I find difficult

Because you see,
Now that I have mentioned pineapples

It is as if all the pineapples in the world
Are in some way significant to me,

Which is not true.

But I actually dislike all watermelons.

I am now listening to another song.

Dale Chou
2013-08-02
