Out again

The heads-on-pikes are out again.  
They've rolled them out

again.  White shirts for
purity; clear skies for hope; 

pink -- femininity and 
care and all that baggage;

green, blue, orange, 
red -- everything is loaded, 

every pun, intended.  
The campaign cars reading names;

Larger than life posters -- 
the dressing game;

Little people wait in
their dirty little chairs --

next round, 
next turn.

Dale Chou
2018-10-24
