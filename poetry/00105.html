A comedian's introduction

I am the king 
Of jesters, master of clowns—
A devious pawn of chess—envious 
Of the sun in my study
(And hated it).

My stern look a joke 
In itself. It cracks the audience
As I wait—puerile, 
For the next cheer.

Dale Chou
2003-02-02
