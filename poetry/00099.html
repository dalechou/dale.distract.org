Confession

Drift -- 
Then to struggle 
Through reality! Oh, how I wished 
To slumber and dream! 
Placid and tranquil it was, 
To yield my mind 
And lull. 
Such contentment it'd be
To return --
To that undisturbed reverie! 
Where my kingdom was complete,
And my palms were held 
Without deceit.

Dale Chou
2002-12-19
