The answer

I am the opposite of being eloquent. 
So if someone should approach and point to 
One of my poems or pictures, all friendly 

And curious, 'What does it mean?' 
Or even, 'Why?'  I can only remain anecdotal, 
Then with some force declare, 'Es muss sein!' 

Because it is, it is; it is.

Dale Chou
