When I find myself at war

When I find myself at war
With the fantastic, I wonder:
     Why?

     And why not?
          Turn and tilt towards the
          Real, and a swish! a slash!
          A fall of arms terrible and old.

                              Behold:
               I have steadied the sail of the
               Big grey mill -- tall against the wind
               Now, is the manifestation of my bold green cloak.

Dale Chou
2012-08-29
