Personal symbols

I have turned you 
into symbols
simple enough for the world to understand.
Simple enough to 
read and conjure
on first-name basis.  You shall be 
my familiar friend, my keeper, 
dark company that I bring everywhere
in the closeness of my pocket.

Dale Chou
2021-08-29

Delphi

I have stolen words 
from divinity, 

appropriated them 
for personal means; 

these sounds form 
not phrases,

but consonants in 
the dark; 

and I remain
inscrutable and

refusing to pass; 
but I am not.  

No,
I am not.

Dale Chou
2020-05-19

Lights off

I switch the lights off, 
then on, then off, 

and then on.  I do it 
one room at a time, 

so I can find 
the dark 

and keep it cornered -- 
stare right into 

its lightless eyes -- 
one, 

one, 
and then another -- 

lonely organs of 
the night -- by the last hour 

they would all shut 
and die.

Dale Chou
2019-05-08

Emeraldcrest
-- dedicated to Cori

The pyramid rose over 
stony bed.  White shells toiled 

amidst alabaster froth.  
Base upon base, 

rock upon rock.  The great emergence 
over sandy lot.  

A gasp -- a cry -- 
an exclamation, "Emeraldcrest!"

The billow came and 
claimed the plot.

Dale Chou
2018-09-08
