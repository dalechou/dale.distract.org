There is still time to sleep

There is still time
to sleep.  There is still

time to sleep,
but the streets are already

waking from its torpor --
its frigid limbs regathered;

a neighbour's chair
set hard against the floor;

the difficult whimper
of the rolled-up door -- whilst I shuffle

gelid shadow in pale 
purple,

optimistic and quiet at
half past four.

Dale Chou

Delphi

I have stolen words 
from divinity, 

appropriated them 
for personal means; 

these sounds form 
not phrases,

but consonants in 
the dark; 

and I remain
inscrutable and

refusing to pass; 
but I am not.  

No,
I am not.

Dale Chou
2020-05-19

Lights off

I switch the lights off, 
then on, then off, 

and then on.  I do it 
one room at a time, 

so I can find 
the dark 

and keep it cornered -- 
stare right into 

its lightless eyes -- 
one, 

one, 
and then another -- 

lonely organs of 
the night -- by the last hour 

they would all shut 
and die.

Dale Chou
2019-05-08

Emeraldcrest
-- dedicated to Cori

The pyramid rose over 
stony bed.  White shells toiled 

amidst alabaster froth.  
Base upon base, 

rock upon rock.  The great emergence 
over sandy lot.  

A gasp -- a cry -- 
an exclamation, "Emeraldcrest!"

The billow came and 
claimed the plot.

Dale Chou
2018-09-08
