Ten days
—dedicated to S

I'm torn between the day to go
And the day to meet again—
Ten days, before we part,
And a hundred more to wait.

But if such commutation
Should bring a closer union—
Then come!—like ebbing waters
Towards one homing confluence.

Dale Chou
2007-02-11
