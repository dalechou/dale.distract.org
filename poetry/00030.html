Seeking

Dazed! Out! 
Of love; and lost, woe
And hurt—I've fallen, you
Didn't find me.

Running in circles, possessed, crazed, 
Spinning, madly—you, touches, 
Voices and thoughts, chain and imprint
Red marks, enslave and enflame the
Darker inside of me.

Hopeless—I am thinking
About you. Fragments and shapes, anything
That I can take home.

You, illusive as the night, 
My dear, love, where are you now?

Dale Chou
2000-10-07
