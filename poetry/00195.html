A list of unrelated things

Here is a list of unrelated things:
The sordid manner of the summer air
And the dilution of the thinning beings --
Their pauper lamentation hoarse and bare;
The sad music of the blackcarrion song
And the great division of ears and heart
Are mostly subumptive, where they belong
To another darker, more desperate art;
The sleepless listener was forcibly set
Right atop of her deafening throne, and there
She described duly, with her alphabet, 
An enormous collection of despair:
The water.  The cat.  The game.  The stranger.
The algor.  The isle.  The dream.  The flicker.

Dale Chou
2009-08-30
