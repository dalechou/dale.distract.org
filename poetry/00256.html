Tincture L

Extract one sliver of soul
Enough to make good tincture; add this
For something more:
- 3 midnight whispers (with undivided attention)
- 5 drops of regressive memory
- 3 specks of dirt without malice
- 1 act of passion in aliquots of equal violence

Then wash
With a legful of spirit of wine
Before passing over blue flame, twice.

Dale Chou
2012-09-28
