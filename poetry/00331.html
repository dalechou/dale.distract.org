Of light and darkness

The world is made up
Of light and darkness.
I am sure of it.

I have my hands cupped
And ready to see
The farthest company.

I expected much from
This bright city:
More colours and intimacy

A man stands perched
On his mezzanine,
Almost singing.

Dale Chou
2013-09-10
