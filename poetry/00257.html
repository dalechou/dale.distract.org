Tamsui

Did you know?—
They had nets to carry stones.
They planted the pockets deep and
Heavy by the bank

Like they would with seeds, but bright,
Under the golden sun,
Under the setting light,
Meeting the white froth of
Reluctant water

As I stood—the world my companion—
In still despondence.

Dale Chou
