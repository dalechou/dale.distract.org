Beautifully crafted creatures

I escape the notions of death
When I look up at the clouds.
We are beautifully crafted 
Creatures that refuse to die

Without cause, without prose,
Like silent, hulking beasts
Heading towards the home in the forest
For a night's good sleep.

Dale Chou
2015-11-14
