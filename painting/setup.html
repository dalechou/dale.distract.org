Dale Chou's setup

I am keeping a record of my setup, which has significantly improved my quality of life. As I am just starting out, there may be better options out there that I do not know. I only hope to share information and make painting more accessible in some small way.

I am not affiliated with these products. This is a record of what I am using, not a recommendation of quality products.

Oil bath

I use Hollywood safflower oil to soak my unused brushes.

Palette paper

I use palette paper to avoid cleaning out the palette.
