I am keeping a record of my process and setup, which has significantly improved my quality of life. As I am just starting out, there may be better options out there that I do not know. I only hope to share information and make painting more accessible in some small way. Nulla dies sine linea.

I am not affiliated with the products listed. This is a record of what I am using, not a recommendation of quality.

Glasses

I am trying this thing where I take advantage of my nearsightedness and take off my glasses to help with blocking out larger spaces of colours. I only put them back on when I work on the details.

Linseed oil

I put it in a plastic bottle with a narrow opening and just squeeze to use.

Oil bath

I use Hollywood safflower oil to soak my unused brushes.

Palette paper

I use palette paper to avoid cleaning out the palette.
